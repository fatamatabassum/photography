A photography space made for hijabi women.

Fatama Focus is a women-centered photography studio created for hijabi women who want to feel seen, respected, and comfortable. Every session is calm, private, and led with intention.

You never have to explain your boundaries here — they’re already understood.

Why this space exists

Many hijabi women feel unseen or uncomfortable in traditional photography settings. Fatama Focus was created to change that. This is a space where modesty, consent, and confidence are prioritized at every step.

Session offerings

Portrait Sessions

Soft, intentional portraits focused on presence rather than posing.

Graduation & Milestones

Celebrate achievements without compromising comfort or values.

Creative Direction

Help with outfits, mood, and story — always modest and respectful.
